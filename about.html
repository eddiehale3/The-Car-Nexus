About Us

Welcome to The Car Nexus! We are an online marketplace dedicated to providing a seamless experience for buying and selling used cars. Our platform ensures transparency and simplicity in every transaction.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an online marketplace for buying and selling used cars, offering verified listings and secure processes. We aim to provide a trusted platform that ensures quality, convenience, and confidence, setting us apart from broader marketplaces.

Founders

Car Nexus was founded by four dedicated individuals: Paul Gao, KM Shafayat Nazrul, Ashraful Islam, and Nafis Wasel. We are all full-time graduate international students majoring in Information Systems at the University of Arkansas. Together, we combine our expertise and passion for technology to create a platform that redefines the used car marketplace.
